About

Wild Tree Tech is a Switzerland-based software engineering consultancy and open source software organisation.

Data Engineering

Wild Tree Tech is based in Brugg, Switzerland. We deliver solid engineering for clients all around the world, offering Software Engineering, Technology consulting and Training. We specialise in building digital products that leverage machine-learning and AI.

Open Source

Wild Tree Tech loves open source. Not only do we use it to implement our client's projects but we have also created and actively contribute to some of the most advanced projects in the PyData ecosystem. These projects include mybinder.org, JupyterHub and scikit-optimize.

Being part of large, international, and open communities means we have a lot of experience managing diverse teams at scale.

Using open source products means client projects have a solid foundation and benefit from the development of new ideas that is happening at light speed.

The Team

Wild Tree Tech is owned and operated by Dr. Tim Head. Before creating Wild Tree Tech in 2016 he obtained a doctorate in experimental particle physics and worked at Fermilab, CERN and EPFL. In his free time he trains for triathlons.

As required Wild Tree Tech engages trusted frontend developers, sales experts and machine-learning engineers in Zurich and Berlin.
