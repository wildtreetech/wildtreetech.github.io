About

Wild Tree Tech builds bespoke solutions for clients all around the world, from startups to UN organisations. Wild Tree Tech operates fully managed data science environments for workshops and lecture courses. It is a Switzerland-based data engineering consultancy that embraces open source software and processes.

Data Engineering

Wild Tree Tech is based near Zurich, Switzerland. We deliver solid engineering for clients all around the world, offering data engineering, Jupyter consulting and training. We specialise in building digital products that leverage machine-learning and AI.

Open Source

Wild Tree Tech loves open source. Not only do we use it to implement our client's projects, we have also created and actively contribute to some of the most advanced projects in the PyData ecosystem. These projects include mybinder.org, JupyterHub and scikit-optimize.

By being part of large, international, and open communities we have a lot of experience managing diverse teams at scale and have insight to the trends of tomorrow.

Using open source products means client projects have a solid foundation, benefit from the development of new ideas, and are not locked in to a particular vendor.

The Team

Wild Tree Tech is owned and operated by Dr. Tim Head. Before creating Wild Tree Tech in 2016 he obtained a doctorate in experimental particle physics and worked at Fermilab, CERN and EPFL. In his free time he trains for triathlons.

Frontend developers, sales experts and machine-learning engineers in Zurich and Berlin are part of the Wild Tree Tech team.
