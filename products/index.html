Wild Tree Tech offer a data science platform that unifies people, code and science across your organisation. The platform is based on widely used Jupyter notebook technology with thousands of users world wide.

Data Science Environments

Based on JupyterHub our data science environment is ideal for teaching a programming workshop or as day-to-day working environment for your team.

Environments come preconfigured with Pandas, TensorFlow, Keras and more. Need more? Environments are customisable, installing additional libraries or tools is easy.

Not just Python! RStudio, Julia, C++ (and more) multi-language environments supported out of the box.

Our Data Science Environments can be deployed on premise for small teams or in the cloud for large scale deployments. It is production ready, the largest deployment we help maintain serves over 100000 users a month.
