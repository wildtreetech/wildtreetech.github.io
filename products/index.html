Products

Wild Tree Tech offer a ready made data science platform that unifies people, code and science across your organisation. The platform is based on Jupyter notebook technology which has tens of thousands of users world wide.

Binderhub

Binderhub turns a git repository into a deployed, live data-science environment with just one click. Ideal if you have many projects with different requirements. It gives your team the flexibility of quickly trying new things and not leaving behind an irreproducible mess.

With Binderhub you can open a git repository in an executable environment, making your code immediately reproducible by anyone, anywhere, from any device with a browser.

Try out the live demo.

Managed JupyterHub

Managed JupyterHub instances can be used to serve notebooks to a class of students, a corporate data science group, or a scientific research group. Managed JupyterHub is ideal for deployments at scale or for sharing your work with external partners. Wild Tree Tech will customise and host your instance for you on the Google cloud.

Supports Python, R Studio and many other languages.

On-premise JupyterHub

Wild Tree Tech will help you setup and customise a JupyterHub deployment on your own compute hardware. On-premise JupyterHub instances are ideal if you have sensitive data or do not need the scalability of a cloud deployment.

Supports Python, R Studio and many other languages.
