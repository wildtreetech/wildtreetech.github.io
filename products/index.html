Products

Wild Tree Tech offer a ready made data science platform that unifies people, code and science across your organisation. The platform supports Python, R Studio, C++ and many more languages. It can be deployed on-premise or in the cloud. Short term setups for a workshop or long term for your organisation.

The platform

Wild Tree Tech's data science platform is ideal for data science teams, fast deployment of apps, and live workshops. Based on the leading Jupyter technology stack which is used around the world by startups, universities, and enterprise users. The platform is based on Jupyter and R notebooks and can be fully customised.

Deployments are available for small, in house teams up to internet scale public clusters. We offer fully managed deployments as well as working with your operations team to run your own instances.

On-premise

Wild Tree Tech will help you setup and customise a deployment on your own compute hardware. On-premise instances are ideal if you have sensitive data or do not need the scalability of a cloud deployment. For teams up to 50 people.

Supports Python, R, C++ and many other languages.

Fully Managed

Managed instances can be used to serve Jupyter and R notebooks to a class of students, a corporate data science group, or a scientific research group. Managed instances are ideal for deployments at scale or for sharing your work with external partners. Wild Tree Tech will customise, host and manage your instance for you.

Supports Python, R Studio and many other languages.

Fully Managed Teaching

Managed teaching instances come with state of the art libraries installed and support Jupyter and R notebooks. Reduces the amount of time spent supporting students getting software setup and lets you focus on the teaching. Course material is automatically distributed to students and homework can be submitted to learning management systems. Wild Tree Tech will host, support and manage your instance for you.

Ideal for courses in Python, R Studio, C++ and many other languages.

Enterprise

Enterprise deployments allow your staff to share their work with a one-click. This dramatically reduces the friction between teams and icnreases knowledge sharing. Ideal for organisations that want fast deployment times of data science tools as a service for internal and external users.

Reproducible Scientific Publishing

Reproducible computational science is a hot topic with many challenges. Wild Tree Tech offer a cloud-based computational reproducibility platform that lets your researchers easily share and discover work. Today's research is based on software, statistical analysis and algorithms that are not included in traditional publishing. They are essential to reproducing the research results and reusing them in a new product or research. Their absence from traditional journal articles is a major obstacle for researchers.

Wild Tree Tech will setup and maintain a user friendly computational research platform for your staff, students and researchers. It has first class support for GitHub as well as Gitlab and supports Python, R Studio, Fortran and many other languages. The paltform is based on open-source tools so you do not end up tied to proprietary software.
